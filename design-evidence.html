Colour Theory

I decided to contrast a darker cooler purple consistently, giving a serene, calming and mysterious aspect to this website. I wanted to establish these moods and feelings through the colour scheme as this compliments not only the pictures I have used but the overall mood I wanted to establish when creating my website beforehand. The use of purple on my website is also contrasted by the use of darker blues for the menu and white for the backgrounds, this helps create a professional look for my website along with allowing each section to be defined clearly. The use of Purple even though it is a secondary colour may be a niche choice compared to other websites that use bold primary colours attracting the viewers eye, though I decided to use this colour as not only does it help set the mood but compliments my images of graffiti. “people decide whether or not they like a product in 90 seconds or less and that 90% of that decision is solely based on colour.” This is why I decided to use my colour scheme as purple, it not only looks attractive and works well with my website but also adds brand recognition.
